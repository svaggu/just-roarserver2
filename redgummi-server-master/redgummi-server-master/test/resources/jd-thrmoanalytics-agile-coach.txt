The Agile SME will be responsible for growing the global Agile Business within Mindtree.  The SME will support Account Managers, Client Partners and Business Development Mangers in Agile transformation and delivery deals.  Candidate must be able to articulate solution positioning to both business and technical users in an Agile model (SAFe, DAD, Scrum, Kanban, etc.). The Agile SME is responsible for actively driving and managing the Agile Maturity evaluation, Sprint 0 and solution definition stages of the sales process, working in conjunction with the sales team as the key Agile and technical advisor and advocate for Mindtree services.


The major responsibilities of this role will include, however are not limited to the following.



    Grow the Agile business portfolio inside Mindtree

    Actively drive and manage large Agile deal pursuits ($5-10M+)

    Understand client requirements, scope levels of effort and design solutions for enterprise Agile transformations

    Ensure complete customer satisfaction through all stages of the sales process

    Presentations to C-level executives in Fortune 500 companies

    Establish and maintain strong relationships with account partners & business stakeholders throughout the sales cycle

    Must be prepared for extensive travel (up to 50%)
