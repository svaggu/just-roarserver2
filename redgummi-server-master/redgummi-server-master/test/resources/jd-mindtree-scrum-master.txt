    The Scrum Masters Key Responsibilities include:



    Facilitate the success of a team in delivering functionality on time, within budget, and with high quality

    Organize, facilitate and lead Scum ceremonies including but not limited to: sprint planning, daily Scrum meetings, retrospectives, release planning, and reviews

    Track and communicate team progress daily

    Track and communicate sprint or release progress as needed or directed by customer

    Facilitate communication and improve transparency

    Encourage discussion, conflict resolution, different approaches or alternatives

    Empower your team to self-organize

    Help mitigate distractions and increase the team’s focus

    Maintain a full understanding of Scrum to ensure Agile and Scrum practices are followed

    Earn respect of the team and take initiative to complete projects

    Function in a highly interactive and open environment

    Manage and mentor a diverse team of developers, testers and business analysts

    Be a servant leader for the team

    Motivate teams to deliver a vision and foster an environment for continuous improvement

    Lead and participate in Agile, Scrum and other training

    Encourage and coach Lean thinking

    Contribute to the center as a part of Delivery Leadership

    Work with internal teams to foster the best client relationship possible

    Support the Product Owner by assisting with backlogs and release plan maintenance

    Coach and mentor other scrum masters to ensure cross-team coordination and consistency
